Hello and welcome to my homepage!


I am a first year student in the Information and Communication Technology programme at HZ University of Applied Sciences.
I chose HZ rather than other universities I applied to because I enjoy living in smaller communities and I also love being around nature, especially the sea, and there's plenty of it here.

Why did I choose this program? - Since I was a kid I've always been around computers. I've spent much time having fun exploring all that computers can do, and I've never been left unfascinated. A job in a workplace surrounded by like-minded people and computers just sounds like a dream. Staying at home and sitting in front of a screen is the most easy-going activity one could come up with and when paired with good colleagues and proper timing for breaks, nothing is better than this.
